[Interpretability of Large Language](https://github.com/mega002/llm-interp-tau)

This repository contains materials for the Interpretability of Large Language Models course (0368.4264) at Tel Aviv University. It is a graduate-level, active-learning course in which students learn about interpretability of LLMs in the style of a collaborative research group. The course is structured around weekly paper readings, in-class discussions, role-playing, and hands-on exercises
